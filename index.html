🛸 Connect Sync

Sync Taylor's board across both your phones using a free Firebase database. One-time setup, takes about 3 minutes.

1Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2Click "Create a project", give it any name (e.g. "taylors-missions"), skip Google Analytics, click Create project.
3In the left sidebar, click Build → Realtime Database. Click "Create Database", choose your region, select "Start in test mode", click Enable.
4Copy the database URL shown at the top — it looks like:
https://taylors-missions-default-rtdb.firebaseio.com
Taylor needs to open the same URL on his phone once, then paste the same Firebase URL. After that, both devices stay in sync automatically. You can also tap "Skip" to use the app without sync.
